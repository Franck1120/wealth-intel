# Commodities & Forex

## Task
I want in-depth commodity or forex analysis with fundamentals, technicals, and macro context so that I can decide whether to go long, short, or flat on a specific commodity or currency pair. Success means: I have a clear directional view with entry, target, stop, and the right instrument (ETF/ETC/futures/physical) to express the trade.

## Your Input
$ARGUMENTS

The user may ask about: a specific commodity, forex pair, gold/silver physical, carry trade, or strategic metals.

## Context
Use web search to find: spot prices, futures curve, inventories, CFTC COT data, USD index, central bank rates.

**For commodities:** spot, front-month futures, contango/backwardation (roll cost!), supply/demand balance, inventories vs 5Y average, production trend, seasonal pattern, COT positioning.
**For forex:** spot, rate differential, PPP fair value, current account, carry/vol ratio.

**Gold/Silver physical (Italy):**
- Spread: bars 1-3%, coins 3-8%
- Italian dealers: Italpreziosi, BullionVault, GOLD Avenue
- Storage: professional vault ~0.1-0.3%/year vs home (theft risk)
- Italian tax: 26% on gain. If no purchase documentation → taxable base = 75% of sale price
- Gold/Silver ratio: historical avg ~60-80. High (>80) = silver undervalued

**Strategic metals (Lithium, Cobalt, Rare Earth):**
No direct access — only via mining ETFs or miner stocks. China controls most of the supply chain = biggest risk.
